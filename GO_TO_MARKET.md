# Go-To-Market

## Product positioning

Data Router Standalone is positioned as a routing orchestration layer for teams that manage high-volume operational data across tools, APIs, and files.

## ICP (ideal customer profile)

- AI-forward startups with fragmented data flows
- Marketing and operations teams routing campaign and CRM data
- SMB/agency teams needing lightweight automation without full enterprise ETL overhead

## Revenue model

- Starter monthly subscription for individuals/small teams
- Growth tier for integrations and increased routing volume
- Enterprise contracts for advanced controls and dedicated support

## 3-year target framing

- Goal: progress to a **$10M annualized revenue trajectory in 3 years** by compounding integration coverage, conversion from free to paid, and enterprise upsell.

## Launch channels

1. Developer-first launch content and demos
2. Vercel-hosted test website for rapid validation
3. Partner channel via integration templates
4. SEO around “data routing”, “workflow orchestration”, and “multi-destination export”

## Competitive notes

- Differentiate from generic automation tools by offering source-to-destination routing intent in a single operational surface.
- Differentiate from enterprise iPaaS by providing faster setup and SMB-friendly pricing.
