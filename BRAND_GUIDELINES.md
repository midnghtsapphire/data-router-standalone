# Brand Guidelines

## Brand essence

- **Name:** Data Router Standalone
- **Promise:** Route the right data to the right destination with less operational overhead.

## Voice and tone

- Clear, operational, and technical
- Confident without hype
- Accessibility-aware and inclusive

## Visual direction

- Dark-first UI with high-contrast highlights
- Clean card-based workflows and clear state indicators
- Priority on readable typography and keyboard-navigable controls

## Messaging pillars

1. Universal routing coverage
2. Fast setup and practical automation
3. Transparent, testable deployment and validation
